## Dataset usage

**Middle branch (temporal detection)**
- DoTA  
- CVPR:ACCIDENT @ CVPR (Kaggle)  

Used for frame-wise accident detection and temporal localization.

**Lower branch (report generation)**
- MM-AU  
- VRU-Accident  
- BDD-X  

Used for draft accident report generation, accident description, cause-related information, and driving-related explanations.
